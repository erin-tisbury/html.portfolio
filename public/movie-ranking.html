Erin's All-time Favorite Movies

A list of movies I could watch over and over!

The Princess Bride

This movie night classic is first on my list for good reason.
This is the ideal movie for any occasion and has the best one-liners.
I have this whole movie memorized so that should tell you how much I
love it! I grew up on this movie and it is one of my comfort movies
when I'm sick. Deep down we all love a good fairy tale where the
handsome guy rescues the beautiful girl, the bad guy is punished (or at
least humiliated), the funny guys have all the best lines and a kid learns a
lesson in love and what is important in life.

Star Wars - The Original Trilogy

I mean, come on, these are some of the most iconic movies of all
time and always will be. I remember watching these on my collector
VHS tapes thinking I would never watch a movie that compared. I also
had a massive crush on Han Solo and my first ever handle was HansGrl00
WAY BACK in the 90's! The love story, the epic battles, the Ewoks,
characters and aliens will always be a good dose of nostalgia for me.

Twister

I realize this movie wouldn't make it in most people's top 100 movies
but I happen to love it! I've always been fascinated with the sky and
storms so this crazy group of storm chasers and their wild ways always
sounds like a good time to me.

Disaster/Apocalyptic Movies

I will group this last set together because I have a weakness for
movies like this. World War Z, San Andreas, Day After Tomorrow,
Jurassic Park (haha!), and others like this. Something about them
makes me realize life right now isn't so bad...maybe that's why I
go back to them over and over!
